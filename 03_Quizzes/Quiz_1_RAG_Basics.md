# Quiz 1: RAG Basics
**After: Introduction to RAG (15 minutes into session)**

**Time Limit**: 3 minutes
**Questions**: 8
**Format**: Multiple choice + True/False

---

## Question 1
**What does RAG stand for?**

A) Rapid AI Generation
B) Retrieval Augmented Generation
C) Random Access Generation
D) Recursive Algorithm Generator

**Correct Answer**: B

---

## Question 2
**Which problem does RAG primarily solve?**

A) Making LLMs run faster
B) Making LLMs smaller
C) Reducing hallucinations and providing up-to-date information
D) Training LLMs from scratch

**Correct Answer**: C

---

## Question 3
**RAG has two main phases. What are they?**

A) Training and Testing
B) Indexing and Query
C) Reading and Writing
D) Input and Output

**Correct Answer**: B

---

## Question 4
**In the Indexing phase, what do we NOT do?**

A) Split documents into chunks
B) Create embeddings
C) Store in vector database
D) Answer user questions

**Correct Answer**: D (Answering happens in Query phase!)

---

## Question 5
**True or False: RAG requires retraining the LLM on new data.**

A) True
B) False

**Correct Answer**: B (False - that's the beauty of RAG! No retraining needed)

---

## Question 6
**Which analogy best describes RAG?**

A) Closed book exam (memory only)
B) Open book exam (can reference materials)
C) Pop quiz (unexpected)
D) Group project (collaborate)

**Correct Answer**: B

---

## Question 7
**What is the role of the vector database in RAG?**

A) Store the LLM weights
B) Store and search embeddings for relevant documents
C) Train the embedding model
D) Generate the final answer

**Correct Answer**: B

---

## Question 8
**In the paper exercise, Sheet B was easier because:**

A) It had fewer words
B) It was printed in larger font
C) Information was organized with metadata (like RAG!)
D) The questions were different

**Correct Answer**: C
