COMPANY OVERVIEW
----------------

ACME Corporation is a technology company founded in 2024, specializing in AI-powered automation solutions. Our headquarters is located in Bangalore, India, with additional offices in Mumbai and Hyderabad. We currently employ 450 people across engineering, sales, and operations teams.

Our flagship product, AutoFlow AI, was launched in March 2024 and has already gained 15,000 active users. AutoFlow AI helps businesses automate document processing, data extraction, and workflow management using advanced machine learning models.


PRODUCT DETAILS: AutoFlow AI
-----------------------------

AutoFlow AI Platform Features:
- Intelligent Document Processing: Extract data from invoices, receipts, contracts, and forms with 98% accuracy
- Multi-language Support: Works with 25+ languages including Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, and English
- Custom Workflow Builder: Drag-and-drop interface to create automation workflows
- API Integration: RESTful APIs with SDKs for Python, JavaScript, and Java
- Cloud Deployment: Hosted on AWS with 99.9% uptime SLA

Pricing Plans (as of January 2025):

- Professional Plan: $199/month - Up to 10,000 documents/month
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing - Unlimited documents, dedicated support

Technical Stack:
- Backend: Python (FastAPI), Node.js

- AI Models: Custom fine-tuned GPT-4, Claude Sonnet, Gemini Pro
